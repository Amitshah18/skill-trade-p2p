Scenario

SkillTrade is an AI-powered platform that transforms the way people exchange skills and knowledge. Using a token-based system, users can teach what they know and learn something new, creating a collaborative and self-sustaining learning environment. The platform’s AI matching system connects users based on their skills and learning interests, ensuring the best teacher-learner pairings. Skill exchange happens through live video sessions, messages, or interactive lessons, making learning more flexible and engaging. Users earn virtual tokens by teaching and spend them to learn, with leaderboards and rewards encouraging participation. A rating and feedback system builds trust and helps users find the best mentors. Additionally, AI-powered features like personalized recommendations and chatbots enhance the learning experience, making SkillTrade an innovative and accessible way to grow and share knowledge
